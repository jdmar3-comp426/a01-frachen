Shang Chi's Biography

WARNING: Potential Spoilers Ahead!

Xu Shang-Chi is a Chinese born mix martial artist. Shang-Chi was born in 1998-1999 to Xu Wenwu and Ying Li. At a young age, Shang-Chi's mother was murdered by a rival gang. Due to his mother's death, Shang-Chi's father sore revenge - "a blood debt must be paid in blood" - and forced his son into mix martial arts training for basically Shang-Chi's entire childhood. He would escape his father's compound at the age of 15 and not return for the next 10 years. Now, Shang-Chi lives a life of thrill and adventure.
